
Red Light can turn subtitles on for you during playback, and can also use subtitle timing to decide when to show Next Episode and Credit Stinger alerts. These are two separate jobs that share the same settings.

All subtitle settings are found in Playback->Subtitles.

The master switch is "Auto Enable Subtitles". When this is off, Red Light does not fetch or enable subtitles for you, but Kodi's own subtitle controls still work as normal.

When it is on, you choose a source with "Auto Enable Subtitle Source"....

LOCAL: Uses only the subtitles already inside the file (embedded/sidecar). Nothing is downloaded. With "Forced Only" selected as the language, only forced tracks are used.
SUBMAKER: Downloads subtitles from SubMaker. Only appears once you enter a SubMaker manifest URL. Can fall back to OpenSubtitles if configured.
OPENSUBTITLES: Downloads subtitles from opensubtitles.com. Only appears once you enter your username and password. Free accounts are limited to around 20 downloads per 24 hours.

"Prefer Local Subtitles" only applies when your source is SubMaker or OpenSubtitles. It tries embedded/sidecar subtitles first, and keeps a subtitle that is already showing rather than downloading another.

"Subtitle Language" sets the language for local track selection and for SubMaker/OpenSubtitles downloads.

ALERT TIMING (SEPARATE)

Some alerts can use subtitle cue timing instead of a percentage. This is used when the alert timing for Autoplay Next Episode, Autoscrape Next Episode, or Credit Stingers is set to "Subtitles Info".

This needs a SubMaker manifest and/or an OpenSubtitles account, otherwise the option is hidden. Red Light fetches a subtitle file early in playback purely for timing, and reads the cue times to decide when to pop the alert. If nothing usable is found, it falls back to Playback Percentage (or chapters where available).

Note: this follows the subtitle file's cues, not the on-screen credits, so timing can vary between releases.

WHAT RED LIGHT DOES NOT DO

Red Light has no in-addon subtitle browser. For manual choice, use Kodi's own player subtitle menu. Red Light does not pull subtitles from other addons.

MAINTENANCE

Tools->Clear Subtitles Cache removes Red Light's temporary downloaded subtitle files. This also runs as part of a broader cache clear.
